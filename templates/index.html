Hold It

A quick and simple placeholder service.

To request a placeholder image of a given width and height simply include an image with the source pointing to /<width>x<height>/ on this server. You can also pass color and text such as:

        <img src="{{ url_for('generate_image_dimensions', dimensions='300x180', _external=True) }}" >
        <img src="{{ url_for('generate_image_dimensions_color', dimensions='300x180', color='4a148c', _external=True) }}" >
        <img src="{{ url_for('generate_image_dimensions_color_text', dimensions='300x180', color='e53935', text='Hello', _external=True) }}" >
